About Circuits.com

Circuits.com is building a searchable global index of the entire circuits industry.

Our mission is simple: connect the people, companies, products, services, and technologies that power the circuits industry through one intelligent search platform.

Whether you’re searching for manufacturers, distributors, engineers, design services, testing, assembly, recruiting, or a specific component, Circuits.com is designed to help you find the right connection, quickly and easily.

The Circuits-Keyword™ Model

What makes Circuits.com different is our Circuits-Keyword™ model.

Every searchable keyword has a permanent ranking based on when a company claims it. The first company to secure a keyword earns the #1 position, the next company earns the #2 position, and so on. Earlier participation secures a higher permanent position, creating lasting digital real estate within the circuits industry.

As more companies join, the directory becomes more valuable for everyone. Buyers gain access to a broader network of suppliers and professionals, while participating companies establish a permanent presence where the industry searches.

Our Vision

Our vision extends beyond products. If it can be searched within the circuits industry, such as companies, people, services, technologies, careers, or solutions; then it can be indexed, organized, and connected through Circuits.com. We’re building more than a directory.

Circuits.com is building a searchable platform that connects the entire circuits industry.
