About Circuits.com

Circuits.com is a supplier directory for the electronics industry. One search bar connects buyers with the companies that stock the parts they need.

How it works

Search any term related to circuits, whether a part family, a category, or a specific keyword, and Circuits.com returns the companies that supply it. There are no fixed categories. You can search for any term, and any business can list itself under the terms relevant to its catalog.

Buyers describe what they need in plain language and see the suppliers who carry it. Every keyword has its own results page with a clear table of companies, including contact names, phone numbers, and email addresses. No account is required.

Sponsor a keyword

Any keyword can be sponsored. Advertisers pay to claim a term and appear in the premium banner at the top of that keyword's results, the first listing a buyer sees. It is a direct way to reach customers who are already searching for what you sell.

Keywords are open. You can sponsor a broad term such as semiconductors, a category such as PMIC, or a term specific to your product line. If a buyer can search it, you can sponsor it.

List your business

Listing is open to any company that supplies circuit parts. Provide your details, select the categories you stock, and your company will appear in the directory for each one. Join here.
