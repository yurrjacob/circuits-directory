About Circuits.com

Circuits.com is a supplier directory for the electronics industry. One search bar connects buyers with the companies that stock the parts they need.

Buyers: Search Away & Find the Parts and Suppliers You’re Looking For

Search any term related to the electronic circuits industry, whether a part number, a part category, or a specific keyword, and Circuits.com returns the companies that supply it. There are no fixed categories. You can search for any term, and any business can list itself under the terms or keywords relevant to the products they sell.

Buyers describe what they need in plain language and see the suppliers who carry it. Every keyword has its own results page with a clear table of companies, including contact names, phone numbers, and email addresses. No account is required.

Suppliers: List Your Business & Get Exposed to Buyers Searching for Specific Parts

Keyword Listings are open to any company in the electronic circuits industry. Just fill out the application on our website List Your Company, and simply select the terms or keywords you are interested in being listed under. John or Mike will then reach out to you in short time.

Once payment is received, your company will instantly appear in the Circuits.com website directory under each term or keyword you wish to be listed under. Companies are listed on a first come first serve basis under each keyword.

Suppliers: Get A Trust Badge & Convey Credibility to Buyers

Add a Trust Badge to your listing to show buyers your company is a trusted source in the electronic circuits industry. Trust badges add credibility, validity and security for potential buyers, indicating that your firm and the electronic components you sell can be trusted.

Suppliers: Sponsor a Keyword & Get Exclusive Premium Banner Ownership

Any term or keyword can be sponsored. Suppliers can pay to claim a term and appear in the premium banner at the top of that keyword’s results — the first listing a buyer sees. It is a clear, direct way to reach buyers who are already searching for what you sell. Search any keyword to see where your Exclusive Premium Banner would be located! Search Keywords

Premium banners are exclusive and are only open to one sponsor per keyword. You can sponsor a broad term such as semiconductors, a category such as PMIC, or a term specific to your product line, such as a part catalog number. If a buyer can search it, you can sponsor it.
